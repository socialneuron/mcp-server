# MCP Example Prompts

Natural language prompts for using Social Neuron through Claude Code or any MCP client.

## Ideation

- "Generate 5 Instagram post ideas about sustainable fashion for Gen Z"
- "What are the trending topics in the tech space right now?"
- "Create a week's worth of content ideas for a fitness brand"

## Content Creation

- "Create a 30-second product launch video"
- "Generate a carousel about our top 5 features"
- "Make an image for our Instagram story about the summer sale"
- "Adapt this blog post for Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram"

## Distribution

- "Schedule this image to Instagram and LinkedIn at 9am tomorrow"
- "Post this video to TikTok right now"
- "What are my best posting times this week?"

## Analytics

- "Show me my best-performing content this month"
- "Check my analytics and suggest improvements"
- "Give me a loop summary of what's working and what isn't"

## Planning

- "Create a weekly content plan for my brand"
- "Quality check this caption before I publish it"
- "Find the next 5 available scheduling slots"

## Management

- "Check my credit balance before generating a video"
- "Review my brand profile and update the voice for LinkedIn"
- "Set up autopilot to post 3 times per week"
- "Show me the comments on my latest Instagram post"

## Workflows

- "Generate 5 ideas, quality check them, and schedule the best one for peak time"
- "Pull my analytics, find what's working, and create similar content"
- "Check my credits, generate a video, and schedule it for tomorrow morning"
